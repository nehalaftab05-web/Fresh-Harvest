🌾 Farm Management System
From Orchards
to Opportunities
A comprehensive digital platform for fruit farmers — track production, manage batches, process orders, and grow your agricultural business.
360°
Lifecycle Coverage
10+
Core Modules
8
User Roles Supported
🌱
Plantation Records
Record crop type, variety, plantation date, field, expected yield, and seed source per field and season.
📦
Batch Management
Auto-generate unique Batch IDs (BATCH-YYYY-NNNN) and track every batch from harvest to dispatch.
🏭
Processing & Grading
Record polishing, sorting, assign A/B/C quality grades, and set batch outcomes: Accepted, Rework, or Rejected.
🏪
Inventory & Storage
Assign packaged batches to warehouse locations with temperature conditions, shelf-life, and reserved quantities.
🧾
Orders & Invoicing
Create confirmed orders with auto-generated proforma invoices; support cash and credit payment terms.
📊
Seasonal Analytics
Generate yield, expense, sales, and inventory reports exportable as PDF and Excel for any season or date range.
