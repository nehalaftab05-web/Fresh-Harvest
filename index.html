Everything Your Farm Operation Needs

From a single dashboard, manage every stage of your fruit business with confidence.

🌱
Plantation Records
Log every field, crop variety, plantation date, and expected harvest. Track seasonal activities like irrigation, fertilization, and pest control.
📦
Batch Management
Create and track batches from harvest through grading, packaging, and dispatch. Auto-generated batch IDs with full lifecycle visibility.
🏭
Processing & Grading
Record washing, sorting, and grading results. Assign Grade A/B/C to batches and flag rejects or rework. Full factory traceability.
🏪
Inventory & Storage
Assign packaged batches to warehouse locations, track stock levels in real time, and get alerts before inventory runs critically low.
🧾
Orders & Invoicing
Manage customer orders, generate invoices automatically, record payments, and maintain a full credit ledger with overdue alerts.
📊
Seasonal Analytics
Compare yield, revenue, and batch performance across seasons. Export PDF and Excel reports to share with stakeholders.
